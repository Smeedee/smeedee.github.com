How can I contribute?

Reporting bugs

In order to create a quality product we are depending on you - our users - to tell us about any errors or bugs you may encounter, request features you find usefull, and generally giving us feedback.

Bugs and feature requests can be posted to our bug tracker on github. <%doc>TODO: URL

If you want to come in contact with the developers for any other reason you can do so through any of the means listed on our contact page.

Code

If you are able to fix bugs yourself or contribute new features we always appreciate that. Feel free to submit patches to our github repository or contact us if you want to become involved in the development of Smeedee.

Other

If you feel your contributions in other areas will be of benefit to the project, such as graphics, design or documentation, feel free to contact us.
